Career Goals

As a sophomore in college it's hard to decide what my career will look like. Whether I decide to go into broadcasting, private sector, or working for the government. However within these last two years of my Bachelors theres a few things that I wish to accomplish before I take off into my career choice. Theres still so much to learn and choices to acknowledge, narrowing it down would be my top priority.

I wish to pursue all different areas in meteorology to get a feeling of what I want to do. I want a position that will let me help anyone. Thats why internships with the NWS or FEMA would be my main goal. Helping directly or indirectly is the career choice I wish to pursue. That can include all sorts of careers though.

Another goal of mine would be looking into the research section of my major, possibly pursuing a Masters degree. At the University of Missouri I have a handfull of mentors and students that could help me deicide as well. If im going to be honest that would be a little further off my career path. As much as I love school, I want out sometime to live my life haha.
